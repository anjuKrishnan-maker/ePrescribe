Many ePrescribing solutions qualify for the MIPPA incentive – Veradigm Medicare Incentive Capture helps you get paid faster.  Based on your prescribing activity, it will determine the correct G-Code that needs to be added to a patient's superbill to qualify for the MIPPA incentive.  Medicare Incentive Capture will vastly simplify the MIPPA reporting process by eliminating the guesswork from G-Code selection and condensing the information for the billing staff ensuring maximum efficiency and faster payment.  This feature can be accessed for any patient or date range providing maximum flexibility for the billers/coders in the practice, and is included for you in ePrescribe™ Deluxe.
