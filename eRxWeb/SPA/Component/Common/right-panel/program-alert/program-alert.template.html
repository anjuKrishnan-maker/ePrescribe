Patient Access Services for specialty medications bundled with electronic prior authorization

ePrescribe has integration with a 3rd party, AssistRx, for patient access services to help address the complexities of prescribing specialty medications. An iAssist Workflow is available for this drug to help you electronically provide additional patient access services such as prior authorization, treatment initiation and training, copay assistance and drug starter kits.

Actual patient access services may vary by patient and medication.

To start using this functionality at no additional cost, please contact our Patient Access Services Program Enrollment team at: iassist@allscripts.com
